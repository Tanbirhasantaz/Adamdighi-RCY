Our Goals

  • Environmental Protection

    Combat climate change by increasing green cover in Adamdighi upazila.

  • Awareness Building

    Educate students and community about importance of trees.

  • Beautification

    Make roadsides, schools, and public places green and beautiful.

  • Fruit Trees for Community

    Plant fruit trees to provide nutrition and income.

Types of Trees We Plant

  • Shade Trees

    Neem, Banyan, Peepal - for roadside and school grounds.

  • Fruit Trees

    Mango, Jackfruit, Guava - for community nutrition.

  • Medicinal Plants

    Tulsi, Aloe Vera, Curry leaves - for health benefits.
