Our Foundation

7 Fundamental Principles

The core values that guide all Red Cross and Red Crescent societies worldwide

The International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement is guided by seven Fundamental Principles that ensure our humanitarian work remains ethical, effective, and universal. These principles unite all Red Crescent volunteers in our shared mission to serve humanity.

1

Humanity

Protect life and health, ensure respect for every human being.

2

Impartiality

No discrimination based on nationality, race, religion, class or politics.

3

Neutrality

Don't take sides in hostilities or engage in controversies.

4

Independence

Maintain autonomy to act according to our principles at all times.

5

Voluntary Service

Voluntary relief movement not prompted by desire for gain.

6

Unity

Only one Red Crescent Society per country, open to all.

7

Universality

Worldwide movement with equal status and shared duties.

"These seven principles unite all Red Cross and Red Crescent volunteers in our shared mission to serve humanity with compassion and dignity."

— International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement
