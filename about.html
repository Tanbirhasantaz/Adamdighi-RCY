About Us

Red Crescent Youth, Adamdighi Upazila

Red Crescent Youth, Adamdighi Upazila is an Upazila team of Red Crescent Youth, Bogura Unit. RCY Adamdighi Upazila team officially working in Adamdighi Upazila from June 2021.

The Red Crescent Movement is an international humanitarian movement with approximately 97 million volunteers, members, and staff worldwide. It provides assistance without discrimination as to nationality, race, religious beliefs, class, or political opinions.

Adamdighi is an Upazila of Bogura District in the Division of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. Our team works tirelessly to serve the local community through various humanitarian activities, including first aid training, disaster response, health awareness programs, and social welfare initiatives.

Our Mission

To serve humanity, protect human dignity, and build resilient communities through voluntary service.
